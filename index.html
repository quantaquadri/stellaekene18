Our Beautiful Love Story

As told by the bride

So I met Ekene in an interesting way. We are both Jehovah's Witnesses and attend the same congregation, so we see each other at the meetings. I used to come in alone and head home immediately after meetings so I didn't get to make any friends till I reconnected with someone I used to know and we became good friends. She encouraged me to be more outgoing and friendly.

She introduced myself and Ekene, and as time went on he tried to get to know me better but I wasn't giving him attention. He was always there but never pushy. When I wasn't feeling well he would check up on me and was very caring. I started observing him and looking forward to his comments at the meetings, I began to get attracted to him but I remained distant.

One day we went out preaching and somehow his partner left him. The sun was really hot and I had a big umbrella so I asked him to join myself and my partner so we finished the field service together, he then he gave me a ride to my car and on getting home I realised I forgot my umbrella in his car and panicked a little coz that was the only umbrella I had.

I called to tell him that I had forgotten my umbrella and he promised to bring it, so I waited and when I didn't hear from him after more than an hour I decided to take a nap. I hadn't slept for long when his call came in and he was downstairs so I went out and retrieved my umbrella from him. He then told me he would like to see me more often so we fixed a date and we talked a lot and got to know each other better.

‌ ‌ I quickly found out that we had a lot in common and I wished I had given him a little more attention earlier but I needed to be sure of his intentions. We started dating on the 1st of December 2017.

By January 2018 I met his family and he met mine within a few weeks. His family loved me immediately and made me feel welcome. On the 25th of February 2018 he popped the question on one knee after serenading me with a song and poem he had written and composed in a studio specially for me and I excitedly agreed to spend the rest of my life with him.

‌ He is perfect for me and I look forward to spending the rest of my life with this amazing man.
