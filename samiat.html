How My Love With My Ayanfe of Life Started...

As told by the groom

So, there was this fateful day ;I was at my school mother’s office i.e. Dr. Alafia at the Department of Zoology and Environmental Biology, Faculty of Science and I saw this cute chick ; all set and good looking – as in… As a cute bruh that I am myself , I said the tesleem to her and the cute and beautiful chikala responded like say she been dey expect me to tesleem . And that was how I first spoke to the chick oo…

As a very good and cute guy wee I be, I came across madam cutie on fb later on oo and then I sent her a friend request to enhance my being a great guy, u know? So, shortly afterwards, she accepted and we also became friends on that platform too. The chick been dey see me at the department wella owing to the fact that we both were in the same department; studying the same course but I was in my 3rd year when she gained admission into the Citadel of Learning.

As it is popularly known that any Almaroof descendants ehn, we do not miss classes, always very cute and beautiful, religious in most reasonable ways, popular (whichever way you want to interpret that), cultured and well-mannered, etc. All these made me very popular amidst my colleagues and friends as well as lecturers’ and all thanks to Allaah. The fact that we’re friends on fb, I do say tesleem to her back then and some good wishes too whenever Facebook notifies me of her birthday. I could also vividly recall that once I’m done with the wishes, I check out her wall just to feed my eyes on something good which is actually harmless. Lol…

I have been in different relationships from 2008 till I started out with my Ayanfe of Life and I could say that it has been a very different experience ever since we started dating July 30th 2017. I must say, it has really been worth it and amazing even with our normal disagreements and back and forth on some key decisions affecting both of us. Alhamdullilahi robil a’aalameen…

Thinking back retrogressively; after my ex decided to call-off our relationship, it was around the time I had my Master’s graduation ceremony and I shared a couple of pictures on fb and one of those who saw it and wished me well was my Ayanfe of Life. She however, did not messaged me publicly or privately via fb, rather, she did on my personal message handle on LinkedIn. She then added her contact in the message. As a smart thinking and cute guy wee I be, I had to add 1 +1 to equate 11 instead of 2 ,so, I saved her contact and was still thinking about asking her out or not since I been dey single .

I would take a couple of time to go through her fb handle just to be sure whether I really wanted to go that route with her or not but Alhamdullilah that I made the right decision to do so and I pray that we both never have any reason to regret the decision. Aameen.

So, there came the courage eventually. I reached out to the chikala say I been wan discuss some very important thingy with her and would like to know when she would be available or chanced for the discussion. I was thinking that she would respond in about a week or so but then, Ayanfe responded in less than three hours like say she been dey expect make I make that awesome move faa.

I was shocked because I wasn’t expecting the response to be quick but I summoned the courage to welcome the suggestion. Then there comes the moment of truth. lol.. She messaged me around 6:30pm or thereabout and told me to she wanted to take a quick power nap but if I don’t mind, she’ll notify me when she wakes. Fast forward to about 2 hours later, she did messaged me stating that she just woke not quite long and that she’s ready to listen to what I have to say.

I was still struggling to gather my confidence and stamina at the same time and also trying to figure what to say and how to put things together in a very clear, distinctive and explicit perspective so that I will not be beating about the bush and wasting un-necessary time whatsoever. Then the Almaroof in me bounced out and evolved gradually owing to the fact that I also made some supplications before commencing the discussion and as the popular saying goes “the rest was history …”
