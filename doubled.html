Our Beautiful Love Story

As told by the bride

Sometime in September 2016, a very dear friend was concerned about my "single " status and asked to hook me up with a "guy". I wasn't interested as I wanted to focus my energy on self development and not relationships.

December that same year, she insisted on playing cupid and shared my BBM pin with him. He chatted me up asking how I was doing and where I was. I replied with sarcasm " I am in Mars ". Lo and behold, we started a conversation on planets and a lot of other random talks.

The friendship grew as he was just consistent, not forcing anything just allowing things to happen naturally even though we were oceans apart. When we finally met physically May 2017, we connected immediately and I had to ask where he had been all my life.

As told by the groom

I got a text from a friend's fiancee who told me she wanted me to meet her amazing friend. I laughed cynically at her zealous description of her friend. That period was a hectic one for me(I was really hustling) and the last thing I wanted was 'across-the-ocean woman drama'.

The first thing I asked for was instagram page (then I went to facebook) before I even bothered texting my Adeola. Lol. Our first conversation started off with a bit of sarcasm, talking about her claiming to be on another planet and I teased her about being some sort of extraterrestrial body living with humans.

I never put a call through until weeks after, on her birthday(christmas)- it was brief, nothing serious and of course met her in a perfect happy mood. Strategic? Nah! I had forgot she told me her birthday is christmas day and I'd slept most of the day as I'd worked through the eve. But again I got reminded by her friend(bless her!).

As I was hustling, I barely remembered any day or date but this reminder was perfectly timed-in fact, divine. That first call heralded a series of follow-up calls and tonnes of texts which have all escalated to this amazing love journey we both are on.

My Adeola is so much more than her friend described, she is indeed out-of-this-world and we are just right for each other. I will chose her over and over again in a million lifetimes.
